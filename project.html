Introduction:

If there is a point on which most economists agree, it is that trade among nations makes the world better off. Yet international trade can be one of the most contentious of political issues, both domestically and between governments.

When a firm or an individual buys a good or a service produced more cheaply abroad, living standards in both countries increase.

Reasons why countries trade

  1. differences in technology
  2. differences in resource endowments
  3. differences in demand

Benefits of international trade

  • More Job Opportunities
  • Improved Risk Management
  • Opportunities to Specialize

Globalization

Globalization is driven by international trade and investment and aided by information technology.It is the process of interaction and integration among the people, companies and governments of different nations

Economic globalization:

Here, the focus is on the integration of international financial markets and the coordination of financial exchange.

Political globalization:

This type covers the national policies that bring countries together politically, economically and culturally.

Cultural globalization:

This aspect of globalization focuses in a large part on the technological and societal factors that are causing cultures to converge.

Types Of A Trade

Internal Trade
Internal trade is also known as Home trade. It is conducted within the political and geographical boundaries of a country. It can be at local level, regional level or national level.
External Trade
External trade also called as Foreign trade. It refers to buying and selling between two or more countries.
